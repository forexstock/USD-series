How it works

Built on trust.
Settled on Stellar.

Every token in the suite shares the same issuer, the same dollar peg, and the same settlement finality — while each plays a distinct role in the ecosystem.

01
Add a trustline

Open your Stellar wallet (Lobstr, Freighter, Solar) and add a trustline using the token code and the issuer address. This takes under a minute.

02
Receive tokens

Once your trustline is established, you can receive or purchase any USD Series token on the Stellar DEX or directly from authorised distributors.

03
Send & settle

Transact in seconds with near-zero fees. Every transfer settles on the Stellar ledger in 3–5 seconds, with full on-chain auditability.

04
Redeem anytime

Each token is 1:1 redeemable for USD. Contact the issuer directly to initiate a redemption back to fiat at any time.
